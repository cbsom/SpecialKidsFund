Our ACCESSIBLE VAN ASSISTANCE PROGRAM

Please let us know if you need a handicapped adapted vehicle. Requests may be for adults or children anywhere within the USA. Contact us via this form with: Details of the type of accessible vehicle you are in need of. Disability involved Financial need Phone number Your location Important: Requests by form submission only; please, do not call. REQUEST A VEHICLE FORM Family must demonstrate that they do not have the resources to afford a vehicle on their own. Although we have been successful in providing a number of used vans for clients in the past; we can make no guarantees as to the availability, time frame or location of the vehicles. Your submission of a request will put your name in process for evaluation. We will only contact you if a van becomes available in your area, no need to contact us after you submit.
